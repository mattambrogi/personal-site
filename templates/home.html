Hey, I'm Matt.

I’m an engineer and product manager interesting in building tools with data and machine learning.

I work as a full stack engineer. In my freetime I am exploring deep learning and building products with LLMs.

At the start of 2022, I took a break from work to attend The Recurse Center, a self directed retreat for programmers. I built web apps, studied machine learning, and learned a lot from other engineers. You can read about my experience here.

Before that I worked at Capital One. I was a part of Capital One’s first cohort of their Product Development Program. During my first rotation I worked to find actionable insights in customer data. During my second rotation I worked as a PM on Capital One’s machine learning platform, where I stayed for a year an a half. My team built tools that enabled data scientists and engineers to experiment with, improve, and deploy models at scale.

I went to the University of Maryland where I designed my own major called Engineering and Product Management. My course work consisted of math, physics, computer science, design, and business.

Outside of all of that I love to go on adventures outside, skateboard, ride my bike, write, and play music. I was lucky enough to take a year off during college and spend time traveling after. I’ve spent a month in each of the following countries: China, Laos, Cambodia, India, Indonesia, Nicaragua.

I host some writing and recent projects on this site. If you'd like to find out more about me, you can find me on Twitter, GitHub, or LinkedIn
